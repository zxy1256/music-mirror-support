Music Mirror Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

Music Mirror is designed for local music practice. The app does not require an account and does not collect personal data from users.

Data Stored on Your Device

Practice playlists, notes, imported media references, and recordings are stored locally on your device. This information is not sent to the developer.

Microphone

Music Mirror requests microphone access only when you choose to record practice audio or a voice note.

Third-Party Content

If you add a YouTube link or other external media reference, that content is loaded from the third-party service you selected and may be subject to that service's privacy practices.
